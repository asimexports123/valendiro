### Example: index-funds (Flagship #21)

| Field | Current | Target |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Quality** | B- | A |
| **Word depth** | ~272 | 1,500–2,500 |
| **Read time** | 1 min (mislabeled) | 8–12 min honest |

**Knowledge gaps:**
- How to open first index fund account (step-by-step)
- Minimum investment amounts by provider type
- Which index to choose (S&P 500 vs total market vs international)
- Tax implications in plain language
- Common beginner mistakes with dollar impact

**Missing entities:** Vanguard, Fidelity, Schwab, S&P 500, Total Stock Market, ETF, NAV, expense ratio, SEC

**Missing relationships:** index-fund → tracks → index; index-fund → competes-with → ETF; index-fund → requires → brokerage-account

**Missing citations:** Bogleheads wiki, SEC investor.gov, provider official pages

**Missing examples:** "$500/month into total market index for 30 years" scenario

**Missing diagrams:** Index fund vs ETF flow; fee compounding chart

**Missing FAQs:** "Are index funds safe?" "Can I lose money?" "Which index fund for beginners?"

**Missing comparisons:** Index fund vs ETF table; Active vs passive table

**Missing learning path links:** ← mutual-fund-fundamentals → fund-selection → retirement-planning
