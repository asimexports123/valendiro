## Kill List (Do Not Do in Next 6 Months)

- ❌ New architecture / engines / phases  
- ❌ Publish to 500+ pages  
- ❌ Entity pages without substance  
- ❌ Expand to new languages  
- ❌ Mobile app  
- ❌ Paywall  
- ❌ Optimize composition/engineering scores  
- ❌ Feature "Strategy Seo" or similar auto-slugs
